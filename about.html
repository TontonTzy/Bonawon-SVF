About Our Parish

Learn about the rich history and mission of St. Vincent Ferrer Parish and how we serve our community.

Our History

St. Vincent Ferrer Parish in Bonawon, Siaton, Negros Oriental has been a beacon of faith and community for generations. Our parish stands as a testament to the devotion of our people and the guidance of Saint Vincent Ferrer, the patron saint of our community.

From its founding to the present day, our parish has been dedicated to serving the spiritual and material needs of our parishioners, fostering a strong sense of community, and promoting the values of the Catholic faith.

We continue to grow and evolve, welcoming new members and deepening our commitment to Christ and to one another.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ and to serve as a welcoming community that nurtures faith, hope, and love through prayer, sacraments, and service to all people.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant parish community rooted in faith, committed to spiritual growth, and actively engaged in the transformation of society according to Gospel values.

Our Values

Faith in Christ • Compassion & Service • Family & Community • Holiness & Prayer • Hospitality & Welcome
